Company HR Policy Document

1. Working Hours
Employees are expected to work 8 hours per day, five days a week.
Flexible working hours are allowed with manager approval.

2. Leave Policy
Employees are entitled to 20 paid leaves per year.
Unused leaves cannot be carried forward to the next year.

3. Remote Work
Remote work is allowed up to three days per week.
Full-time remote work requires HR approval.

4. Code of Conduct
Employees must adhere to professional conduct and ethical standards.
Harassment or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.
